# Public copy — Plain-English descriptions (3 sentences each)

RoadOS
RoadOS is a compact browser-based desktop that runs in one tab. It keeps tools, files, and small apps together so you can start working immediately without complex setup. Prototype — features are experimental and we want feedback.

RoadCode
RoadCode provides an in-browser editor, live preview, and easy routing for small projects and experiments. It focuses on fast feedback and low setup so creators and students can ship prototypes. Prototype stage — integrations are limited and we welcome testing.

RoadTrip
RoadTrip is a shared space for humans and the BlackRoad agents to coordinate tasks, suggestions, and decisions. It keeps conversations, agent outputs, and simple workflows together so teams can move faster. Planning stage — designs and agent roles are being defined.

PitStop
PitStop provides approachable learning tools: short lessons, quizzes, and guided help from agents. It aims to help students and lifelong learners get unstuck quickly without complex course systems. Planning stage — content models and safety policies are being designed.

RoadWork
RoadWork provides simple, human-friendly tools for tasks, approvals, and governance flows. It's aimed at small teams that want clear processes without heavy tooling. Planning stage — core flows and permissions are being sketched.

BackRoad
BackRoad makes it straightforward to publish content, host discussions, and build community. It prioritizes clear conversations, real comments, and slow-growth community features. Planning stage — community rules and moderation tools are being designed.

CarKeys
CarKeys handles identity, device claiming, and access permissions in a simple, auditable way. It’s built to be understandable — clear ways to claim devices and manage who can do what. Planning stage — security design and user flows are in progress.

RoadBook
RoadBook focuses on clear, verifiable writing and notes with simple peer review. It's designed for researchers, teams, and writers who want readable, citation-friendly publishing. Planning stage — workflows and review rules are being defined.

RoadWorld
RoadWorld offers simple tools to make small, walkable virtual rooms and maps for communities and games. It focuses on approachable building blocks rather than complex engines. Planning stage — mapping tools and privacy rules are being planned.

RoadView
RoadView provides clean dashboards, logs, and search for projects and products. It's designed to show clear signals and verification rather than noisy metrics. Planning stage — data connectors and dashboards are being scoped.

RoadChain
RoadChain records hashes, receipts, and simple audit trails so teams can verify what happened and when. It’s meant for transparency and basic compliance, not for replacing legal records. Planning stage — storage and privacy safeguards are being designed.

RoadSide
RoadSide is a simple support surface to route questions and help requests to the right team or agent. It aims to make getting help quick, human, and traceable. Planning stage — routing rules and integrations are being defined.

RoadCoin
RoadCoin is a non-financial credits layer for contributions, usage, and access control within BlackRoad. It rewards activity and can gate features, but it is not currency for real-world exchange. Planning stage — rules and limits are being worked out.

CarPool
CarPool helps teams collaborate on projects with shared workspaces, simple roles, and a request routing system. It’s built for multi-person and multi-agent coordination without heavy project management overhead. Planning stage — permissions and routing policies are being designed.

BlackBoard

OneWay
OneWay accepts data into BlackRoad in a controlled, one-way way so external systems can feed content safely. It’s designed to avoid accidental data leaks while making import simple. Planning stage — connectors and safety checks are being planned.

RoadBand

HighWay

RoadSport

RoadShow
