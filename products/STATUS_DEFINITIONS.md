# Status Definitions

## product_status
- Planning: Product is in planning or scoping.
- Prototype: Early working demos or experiments.
- Active build: Development work is actively ongoing.
- Live: Product is publicly available and supported.
- Archived: Product is no longer maintained.

## page_status
- Unknown: Page availability not yet checked.
- Not live: No public page exists.
- Landing live: Marketing/landing page is live.
- Demo live: Live demo of product available.
- App shell live: Page or app shell loads; functionality not manually verified.
- App live: Full app/service appears functional (set only after manual verification).
- Redirects: Page redirects elsewhere (needs review).
- Error: HTTP error or inaccessible.
